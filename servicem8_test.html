ServiceM8 Scope Tester

Tests publish_job_notes and manage_attachments scopes against your ServiceM8 account.

Credentials

Step 1 — Fetch a Job UUID pending

Calls GET /api_1.0/job.json to retrieve your first job. If this fails with 401, your credentials are wrong. If it returns jobs, we grab a UUID for the next tests.

Step 1b — Fetch Staff UUID pending

JobActivity records require a staff_uuid. This fetches your staff record automatically.

Step 2 — Test publish_job_notes pending

POSTs a test note to the job diary. A 200/201 response means the scope is approved. A 403 means it's not.

Step 3 — Test manage_attachments pending

POSTs a minimal attachment record to the job. A 200/201 means the scope is approved. A 403 means it's not.
