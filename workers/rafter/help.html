Sign in

The first time you use Rafter on a device, you'll need an invite — your admin sends one to your email. Open the link, confirm your identity (Face ID, fingerprint, or however your device verifies you), and you're set up.

After that, Rafter recognises you when you open the Quote Builder. The same identity check runs automatically — no password, no login screen.

If you switch to a new device, you'll need a fresh invite for it.

Build a quote

Open Rafter and sign in.

The form has four sections, top to bottom.

Customer & job details

Start typing the customer's name. If they're already in ServiceM8, they'll appear in a dropdown — choose them and the name and address fill in. Phone and email need to be entered separately.

If the customer's new, type their details straight in. ServiceM8 creates them when you submit. Site address has address lookup built in — start typing and choose from the suggestions.

Works

Choose the sections this job needs from the chips at the top — Excavation, Paving, Turf, or whatever your business is set up with. Each one you add opens as a card.

Works description comes pre-filled with the standard wording for that section. Edit it to match the job — this text goes onto the PDF and the ServiceM8 job note. Reset puts the original wording back if you need it.

Search materials to add line items — type in the Search materials box, pick what you need, and set the quantity. The section price builds up from the items automatically, but you can type over it to set a fixed figure. Most sections start with a Labour line already in place.

Photos

Add photos opens the photo library for that section, sorted so you're only seeing what's relevant. Choose the photos for this quote. Selected photos appear as thumbnails below the section, and you can remove any of them individually.

Notes

Optional. Anything site-specific — caveats, access instructions, whatever the customer or your team needs to know.

Totals & payment

Subtotal, GST, and total calculate on their own from the section prices. The payment schedule selects itself based on the total — the split changes at different job-value bands. Nothing to enter here.

Preview and send

Preview PDF opens the finished quote in a new tab. Nothing is sent — check it reads the way you want. Close the tab and make changes if needed.

When it is ready, two options:

Submit & Send Quote
Creates a job in ServiceM8, attaches the PDF, and emails it to the customer. The customer's email address is validated before it sends.
Submit Job Only
Creates the same ServiceM8 job and attaches the PDF, but doesn't email the customer. Use this when you want to review the job in ServiceM8 first, or send the quote yourself later.

After submitting, a confirmation screen shows the quote name (e.g. Henderson_6Jun26_v1), the customer, total, and date. From there you can return to the quote or start a new one.

Change a quote you've sent

Open the Quote Builder and choose Find quote. Your recent quotes are listed with the customer name, address, version, and total. Search by customer, address, or quote reference if you need to narrow it down.

Choose a quote and the form opens exactly as it was — every section, item, quantity, photo, and note. A banner at the top confirms which quote you're amending and that re-sending creates a new version on the same ServiceM8 job.

Make your changes. The quote name in the header updates to show the new version number. Then submit the same way — Submit & Send Quote or Submit Job Only. The new PDF replaces the previous one on the ServiceM8 job.

If the ServiceM8 job has been deleted since the quote was sent, Rafter tells you: you'll need to create a new quote instead.

If something isn't working

▸"Operator not found"
The web address doesn't match a Rafter business. Check the URL you were given.
▸Sign-in prompt doesn't appear
Reload the page. If it still doesn't appear, your device may not be enrolled. Ask your admin for a fresh invite.
▸"You need a Rafter invitation from your admin"
Your device isn't set up yet. Ask your admin to send an invite to your work email.
▸"This account doesn't have access to this operator"
You're signed into the wrong business. Choose Sign out & try a different sign-in and try the correct one.
▸"Prices may be out of date"
Rafter couldn't load current materials. Tell your admin — they can check the ServiceM8 connection in Settings.
▸Submit failed
Try once more. If it fails again, contact your admin.

Getting to Settings

Open the Quote Builder and look for the gear icon in the header, next to Help. That's Settings — it only appears if you're an admin. If you don't see it, you don't have admin access; ask whoever set up the account.

Settings has three zones: Sections & photos (what appears in the Quote Builder), Business Configuration (what appears on your quotes), and Team access (who can use Rafter).

Sections & photos

Sections come from your ServiceM8 job templates. To add, rename, or remove a section, make the change in ServiceM8 first, then come back here and click Sync from SM8. Rafter mirrors what ServiceM8 has — it adds new templates, updates changed ones, renames them in place (photos follow the rename), and removes deleted ones. If ServiceM8 returns nothing (token issue, network problem), the sync refuses to run and tells you why. Your sections stay untouched until you try again.

After a sync, a report shows exactly what changed: added, updated, renamed, removed.

Ordering sections

The order you set here is the order everywhere — the Quote Builder's section chips, the photo picker, and the PDF. Drag sections to reorder. Drag only works when all sections are collapsed; expand a section to work on its photos, collapse all to reorder.

Managing photos

Expand a section to see its photo grid. From here you can add photos (click the button below the grid, pick files from your device — they upload and compress automatically), delete photos (hover for the delete icon, or select multiple and use Delete selected), move or copy photos to a different section, and drag photos within a section to set the order they appear on quotes.

Business Configuration

Eight cards, each saving independently. Open one, make changes, hit Save. A failed save on one card never blocks another.

Business details

Company name, ABN, phone, business address, business email, and operator email. The business email appears on PDF quotes. The operator email is internal — it receives quote notifications. ABN validates as you type and checks against the Australian Business Register when you leave the field.

Bank details

Account name, BSB, and account number. These appear on every PDF quote so customers can make direct transfers. BSB formats automatically as you type.

Payment thresholds

Six tiers by job value (Under $20K through to Over $200K). Enter the payment split for each as comma-separated percentages — for example, 20, 60, 20 means 20% deposit, 60% progress, 20% final. The sum indicator next to each tier turns green when it hits 100%.

The Quote Builder uses these to auto-select the payment schedule based on the quote total.

Terms & conditions

Shown at the bottom of every PDF quote. Add clauses one at a time, or click Paste T&Cs text to drop in a full block — each paragraph (separated by a blank line) becomes its own clause. Reorder or delete individual clauses as needed.

Credentials

Licences, insurance, memberships, guarantees — anything that belongs in the "You can rely on" section of the quote. Each entry has a name (e.g. "Fully Licensed & Insured") and an optional detail line (e.g. "Public liability up to $10M").

Email template

The email your customers receive with the quote attached. Use the editor to write and format the body — bold, italic, lists, links. Press Enter for a new paragraph.

Click merge tags to insert placeholders that fill in automatically when the email sends: {client_name}, {job_address}, {quote_ref}, {total}. Use Preview to see how it looks with sample data substituted.

Leave the template blank and Rafter sends a default email with your logo.

Branding

Choose one of eight preset colour palettes, or set custom colours (primary, accent, background) with hex values. The live preview shows how the colours look together. Your choice applies to every PDF quote.

Reset to default clears any preset or custom selection and reverts to the platform palette.

ServiceM8 connection

Shows the health of your ServiceM8 link:

Green — connected, synced in the last day. Working as expected.

Amber — connected, but a sync or two has been missed. Usually resolves on its own; if it lingers, reconnect.

Red — disconnected or no successful sync in three or more days. Reconnect to fix.

Materials and templates sync automatically once a day. If the status goes amber or red, click Reconnect ServiceM8. Rafter sends you to ServiceM8 to approve access, then brings you straight back to Settings with a confirmation.

Team access

Inviting someone

Pick a staff member from the dropdown — it's pulled from your ServiceM8 staff list. Their email fills in automatically. Click Send invitation. They'll receive a link with instructions to complete sign-up. About thirty seconds.

Only staff who are in your ServiceM8 roster and have an email on file appear in the dropdown. If someone's missing, add them in ServiceM8 first (with an email), then hit the refresh button at the top of Team access.

If the invitation email doesn't arrive, Rafter shows a copyable link so you can send it to them directly.

Removing someone

Click the delete icon next to their name in Active members and confirm. They lose access to this business immediately. Two rules: you can't remove yourself, and you can't remove the only admin.

Revoking a pending invitation

Click the delete icon next to the pending invite and confirm. The link stops working immediately. Send a new one if needed.

If the staff list won't load

The dropdown says "Couldn't load your SM8 staff roster" — this usually means the ServiceM8 connection needs reconnecting. Open the ServiceM8 connection card in Business Configuration and reconnect from there.

If something isn't working

▸Settings page redirects to a sign-in screen
Your session expired and Settings can't use your passkey from here. Open the Quote Builder first (passkey works there), then click the gear icon to come back to Settings.
▸"Admin access required"
You're signed in but not as an admin. Ask whoever set up the account.
▸"Couldn't load team data"
Refresh the page. If it persists, the ServiceM8 connection may need reconnecting — open Business Configuration → ServiceM8 connection.
▸"Sync from SM8" says 0 templates or won't run
Rafter won't wipe your sections when ServiceM8 returns nothing — it's a safety check. Usually a token issue. Reconnect ServiceM8 and try the sync again.
▸Staff member missing from the invite dropdown
They need to be in your ServiceM8 staff list with an email address. Add them in ServiceM8, then refresh Team access.
▸Something else
Contact your account administrator.
